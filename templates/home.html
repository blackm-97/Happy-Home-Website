What is Happy Home Badge Hunt?

Happy Home Badge Hunt (HHBH) is a game created in Roblox by veteran Roblox user Tom284. The game was first created in 2009, but has since been expanded into a evergrowing universe that branches between realms! Every corner of the game has a challenge to overcome, and a badge to collect. It also features a colorful and unique cast of characters, and expansive world building! So, don't be afraid to explore! Something to discover awaits between the blocks of every realm.

Tons of Badges to Collect!

The HHBH universe has oodles of Badges to collect, all with their own set of challenges. They can range from being a walk in the park, to soul-crushingly difficult. Keep your wits about you, and don't be afraid of exploring outside the boundaries!

Want to see your progress? Use the Badge List page to see what badges you might be missing!

A Unique and Engaging Story

HHBH contains a colorful cast of characters, and gripping world building that defies expectations. There is lore to discover scattered across the lands connected by the Nexus. See if you can piece together the story for yourself! Most characters will be very talkative, see if you can learn more from them?
